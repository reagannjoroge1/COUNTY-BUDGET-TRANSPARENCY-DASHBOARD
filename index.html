What You Can Do
Step 1 · Safe
Always demand an official receipt
Every county payment must come with a printed official receipt. If refused, photograph the agent and note badge number.
SafeImmediate
Step 2 · Safe
Use eCitizen or M-PESA paybills only
Digital payments create automatic records that cannot be pocketed.
SafeDigital trail
Step 3 · Anonymous
File a report with EACC
EACC accepts anonymous reports. Call 0800 720 750 toll-free. Use the Report tab to build a structured complaint.

Engage your MCA
Your MCA has a legal duty to oversee county spending. Demand public budget hearings.

Submit FOI requests
Under the Access to Information Act 2016, you can request any county revenue report, tender, or contract.
